Subject: DUSG/RES 17 Fixes

Alright folks,I got it working tonight!
I went through the trigger section on the PCB with a fine toothed comb and found that there were two issues:

1) there's a .1 uF cap between the trigger input and V+, right between the 33 K resistor and the two diodes. Dunno why that's there, but I took it out. It's not on Dean's nor Driscoll's schematic.

2) The 5.6V zener diode is connected between the end pulse output and pin 8 of the LM3900. I suspect that Ken's intention was to hook it to pin 7, i.e. ground.

Once I corrected both of those bugs, the module oscillated quite well. VC control over the slopes is excellent for waveshaping. I totally understand why Mr. Driscoll added separate CV inputs for up slope and down slope.

The only thing that isn't working yet is the LED, but that's really minor.I posted a JPG (res17_errata.jpg) to the files area showing where the components are that need to be moved.
